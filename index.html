Backpacking

Backpacking can be lots of fun, as long as you have the right tools. There are countless stories of Backpacking expierences that have failed miserably. If you dont want to become one of those horrible, yet funny stories then make sure you have the right supplies.



Packing Check List

  1. LOTS of dried food, for dinner
  2. Dry Food, for lunch (like Nuts, Dried fruit, Jerky etc.)
  3. Breakfast food (Bars, Oatmeal packets)
  4. A small, lightwieght tent
  5. Warm sleeping bag
  6. Backpacking stove
  7. Fuel for Stove
  8. Small Backpacking Pots
  9. Utensils
  10. Lighter and Matches
  11. Whistle
  12. Warm clothes, non-cotton
  13. Sturdy Hiking Shoes
  14. Water filter (aquatabs or straws)
  15. Drink Mixes for water
  16. Compass
  17. Sun protection, sunscreen/sunglasses
  18. Flashlight w/ batteries
  19. First Aid Kit
  20. Knife/Multitool
  21. Refective Blanket
  22. Nylon cord
  23. Optional: Sleeping pad/Smallpillow
